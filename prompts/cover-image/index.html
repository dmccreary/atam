    A professional textbook cover illustration in flat vector style for a book titled
"The Right Database: Architecture Tradeoff Analysis for Distributed and
High-Availability Systems."

COMPOSITION:
The cover is portrait-oriented (6×9 book aspect ratio). The left third of the
cover features Dex the Robot in a confident, welcoming pose — one arm raised
slightly as if gesturing toward the visual content on the right. The right two-
thirds of the cover shows an interconnected network of six floating database
cylinders, each a distinct color (relational: dark-blue; analytical: teal;
key-value: amber; column-family: purple; graph: green; document: coral),
connected by clean glowing lines that suggest a distributed architecture graph.
A faint hexagonal grid pattern covers the background, evoking a circuit board or
system architecture blueprint.

DEX THE ROBOT (left side, full height, slightly larger than icon scale):
A flat vector cartoon illustration of Dex the Robot — a small, compact, rounded
robot with a steel-blue (#4682B4) body and dark-orange (#E65100) accent details.
Dex has a small antenna topped with a tiny database cylinder, an orange chest
panel with a simple bar-chart icon, and a compact tool belt holding a wrench and
a stylus. Dex's face shows two glowing LED dot-eyes forming an upward arc smile
and a small rectangular mouth display with a warm pixel smile. Dex's right arm is
raised in a welcoming gesture toward the network diagram on the right side of the
cover, as if saying "Let's analyze the tradeoffs!" The left arm holds a small
clipboard or stylus, referencing the ATAM evaluation process.

BACKGROUND:
Deep dark-navy background (#0D1B2A) with a subtle hexagonal grid in slightly
lighter navy (#1A2E45). The distributed database node diagram in the right
portion glows softly, with thin light-blue connector lines. The overall mood is
precise, technical, and intelligent — not cold, because Dex's warm expression
anchors the human element.

COLOR PALETTE:
- Background: #0D1B2A (dark navy)
- Dex body: #4682B4 (steel blue)
- Dex accents: #E65100 (dark orange)
- Title text: white (#FFFFFF)
- Subtitle text: light gray (#B0BEC5)
- Accent lines: #4FC3F7 (light blue glow)
- Database nodes: six distinct colors as listed above

TYPOGRAPHY (describe placement only — do not render actual text in the scene,
add text in post-production):
Top of cover: series or author name in small caps. Center-upper: large bold
sans-serif title "The Right Database." Below title: smaller weight subtitle
"Architecture Tradeoff Analysis for Distributed and High-Availability Systems."
Bottom strip: dark-orange banner with author name and a small "Dex" icon.

STYLE: Flat vector, clean edges, minimal shading, professional textbook quality.
No photographic elements. No gradients except subtle radial glow on the database
node diagram. No text rendered in the image itself.

    Design Notes

    • Dex faces right (toward the content), naturally drawing the reader's eye across the cover from left to right.
    • The six database cylinders mirror the six paradigms covered in the book.
    • Dark navy background differentiates this from the teal Manning cover and reads as modern/technical.
    • The hexagonal grid evokes distributed systems architecture without being literal or cluttered.
    • Typography is kept out of the image prompt so it can be composited cleanly in post-production at any size.
